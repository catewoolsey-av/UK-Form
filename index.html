Investment Professional - Statement

Article 19 Requirements: To be classified as an "Investment Professional", you must satisfy one of the following requirements.

a. Whose ordinary business activities involve them carrying on the controlled activity to which the communication relates for the purpose of a business carried on by them; or

What happens after you submit?

Once you click "Submit Qualification" below, we will initiate the formal documentation process required by UK regulations:

  1. DocuSign Envelope: A DocuSign envelope will be sent to your email address containing your official Financial Promotion Order (FPO) qualification statement.
  2. Review and Sign: Please check your inbox and carefully review the statement. You must complete the electronic signature within 30 days to activate your qualification.
  3. Access Granted: Once the document is signed, you will be able to receive financial promotions and access investment opportunities on the Alumni Ventures platform.
