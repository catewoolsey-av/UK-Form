Risk Warning

Alternative investments in private placements, and private equity investments via feeder funds in particular, are speculative and involve a high degree of risk and those participating investors risk losing all of the capital they invest. Even if an investment performs as projected, private equity is highly illiquid and those investors who cannot hold an investment for the long term (at least 10 years) should not invest. Prospective investors should carefully consider the risk warnings and disclosures before making an investment.

The value of an investment may go down as well as up and investors may not get back their money originally invested. Past performance is not necessarily a guide to future performance.
